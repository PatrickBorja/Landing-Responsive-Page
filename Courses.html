Courses

Attending this school offers an exceptional educational experience that surpasses any other institution. With a faculty of highly qualified and dedicated educators, state-of-the-art technology, and a diverse student body, this school provides an environment that fosters growth and success. The school's commitment to offering innovative and challenging academic programs, unparalleled student support services, and numerous extracurricular activities ensures students leave well-prepared for future success. Overall, this school provides an unparalleled opportunity for academic, personal, and professional growth, making it the perfect choice for those who seek excellence in their education.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Theater and Dance

Majoring in Theater and Dance provides students with the opportunity to explore the arts through a variety of artistic mediums. Through creative expression and performance, students develop skills such as critical thinking, collaboration, communication, and self-discipline. The curriculum typically includes courses in acting, dance technique, stagecraft, and theater history, among others. Graduates are prepared for careers in performing arts, as well as other careers that value skills in creativity, communication, and collaboration. Additionally, the study of theater and dance can enrich one's personal life, providing opportunities for cultural enrichment and self-expression.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Film

A Bachelor of Arts degree with a major in film provides students with a comprehensive understanding of the film industry and its key components, including cinematography, editing, directing, screenwriting, and producing. Students gain hands-on experience through film production projects and internships, and may explore different genres and styles of filmmaking. Graduates may pursue careers in various areas of the film industry, such as film production, distribution, marketing, or film criticism, or they may choose to continue their education in graduate programs.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Visual Communication

The Bachelor of Arts program in Visual Communication focuses on developing students' skills in creating visual content such as graphic design, photography, and videography. Students will learn to use various design software, as well as principles of composition, color theory, and typography. The program also includes courses on communication theory and marketing, enabling graduates to create visual content that effectively communicates ideas and messages to a wide audience. Graduates of this program will be prepared for careers in advertising, media production, and graphic design.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Art and Art History

The Bachelor of Arts Major in Art and Art History program focuses on developing critical thinking and cultural awareness skills through the exploration of traditional and contemporary art forms. With an emphasis on historical context and visual analysis, students will be equipped to analyze and interpret works of art. The program also includes hands-on studio experience, allowing students to experiment with various mediums and techniques to express themselves creatively. Graduates are prepared for careers in arts and cultural organizations, museums, galleries, education, and more.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Music

The Bachelor of Arts Major in Music is a degree program that focuses on the development of skills and knowledge in the field of music. Students in this program learn the fundamentals of music theory, composition, performance, and history. They also have the opportunity to specialize in a particular area of music, such as vocal or instrumental performance, music education, or music technology. Graduates of this program can pursue careers in various fields related to music, including music production, education, performance, and research. Some may choose to continue their studies in graduate programs in music or related fields.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Fashion Design

The Bachelor of Arts Major in Fashion Design is a comprehensive program that prepares students for a career in the fashion industry. Through a mix of theoretical and practical coursework, students learn about design principles, textiles, garment construction, and fashion business practices. They are also given the opportunity to develop their creativity and hone their technical skills. Graduates of this program can pursue careers as fashion designers, product developers, textile designers, fashion buyers, and more. They are equipped with the necessary knowledge and skills to succeed in a dynamic and exciting industry.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Architecture

The Bachelor of Arts Major in Architecture degree program provides students with a comprehensive understanding of the design, construction, and planning of buildings and other physical structures. In this program, students gain knowledge in areas such as architectural history, theory, drafting, computer-aided design, and sustainability. Graduates of this program can pursue careers as architects, architectural designers, construction project managers, urban planners, and more. With a strong emphasis on creativity, innovation, and problem-solving, the Bachelor of Arts Major in Architecture equips students with the skills needed to succeed in the dynamic field of architecture.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Literary Arts

A Bachelor of Arts degree in Literary Arts is a program designed for students who are passionate about reading and writing. This program allows students to explore and analyze different types of literature, including poetry, fiction, and non-fiction, and develop their own writing skills. Students gain a deep knowledge of literature and learn to write critically about it. They also gain valuable communication and analytical skills, as well as a strong foundation in research and writing. This degree prepares students for careers in publishing, journalism, education, or any field that values strong writing and critical thinking skills.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Mass Communication

The Bachelor of Arts Major in Mass Communication program is designed to prepare students to work in various fields of media such as journalism, advertising, broadcasting, and public relations. Students will acquire skills in writing, research, critical thinking, media production, and communication strategies. Graduates can pursue careers as reporters, editors, producers, media managers, advertising executives, and public relations specialists. With the continuous growth of digital media, the program also provides training in digital communication and multimedia production. The program offers a comprehensive curriculum that integrates theoretical knowledge with practical skills, providing students with a well-rounded education in the field of mass communication.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Philosophy

A Bachelor of Arts degree with a major in philosophy equips graduates with critical thinking, communication, and argumentative skills. Through studying philosophical theory, ethics, and logic, students learn to analyze and evaluate complex issues and perspectives. Graduates pursue careers in law, medicine, education, non-profit organizations, and government agencies. The degree also prepares students for postgraduate studies in fields such as philosophy, theology, and social sciences. Ultimately, a degree in philosophy cultivates intellectual curiosity and fosters a lifelong pursuit of knowledge and understanding.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Psychology

The Bachelor of Arts Major in Psychology is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the human mind and behavior. Through coursework, students gain knowledge in areas such as developmental psychology, abnormal psychology, social psychology, and neuroscience. This program equips students with critical thinking skills, research methods, and theoretical perspectives to prepare them for careers in fields such as mental health, human resources, education, research, and more. Graduates with a Bachelor of Arts Major in Psychology have the skills to analyze and interpret human behavior, understand the complexities of mental health, and apply their knowledge to real-world situations.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Political Science

A Bachelor of Arts major in Political Science degree prepares individuals for careers in law, education, public service, business, journalism, and political campaigns. It allows students to develop an understanding of the political system, including government structures, policies, and processes. Students will learn how to analyze political systems, make informed decisions, and communicate effectively with diverse audiences. Graduates will be equipped with critical thinking skills essential for success in many fields and will gain a deep appreciation for the importance of civic engagement and participation in society.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Economics

A Bachelor of Arts major in economics prepares students to analyze and understand how societies allocate resources to meet their needs and wants. Students develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills through courses in microeconomics, macroeconomics, econometrics, and economic theory. Graduates can pursue a broad range of careers in finance, consulting, government, education, and research, or can further their education by pursuing a master's or doctoral degree in economics. The degree provides a strong foundation for students interested in understanding and solving complex economic issues facing society.

Bachelor of Arts Major in Mathematics

The Bachelor of Arts majoring in mathematics program equips students with a solid foundation in mathematical reasoning, analysis, and problem solving. It covers a broad range of topics including calculus, algebra, geometry, statistics, and computer science, providing graduates with skills and knowledge that are in demand across a variety of industries. Graduates may pursue careers in finance, education, data analysis, research, and more. They can also continue their studies in graduate programs such as mathematics, engineering, physics, economics, and computer science. This program prepares students for a variety of dynamic and rewarding careers in the rapidly changing world.

Bachelor of Arts Major in PE

The Bachelor of Arts major in PE focuses on the study of human movement and physical activity. This program aims to develop students' knowledge and skills in various areas such as fitness, sports, health, and physiology. Graduates of this program can pursue careers in teaching, coaching, fitness training, and sports management. They can also pursue further education in related fields such as physical therapy and sports medicine. With a Bachelor of Arts degree in PE, students can promote healthy living and help individuals achieve their health goals.
