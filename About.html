Vision

Our vision for the school is to create a supportive and inclusive learning environment that empowers students with the knowledge and skills required for success in their future careers. We strive to foster a love of learning and curiosity in our students by providing challenging, yet engaging experiences that encourage critical thinking and creativity. Our school values diversity and embraces cultural differences, creating a community of learners who appreciate and respect each other's backgrounds. We aim to equip students with the necessary tools to be responsible citizens and leaders who positively impact their communities and society as a whole.

Mission

Mission of a school is to provide a safe and nurturing environment where students can receive an education that prepares them to become responsible and productive members of society. This includes teaching academic subjects such as math, science, and language arts, as well as instilling values such as respect, integrity, and citizenship. The goal is to create a well-rounded individual who is equipped with the skills and knowledge to succeed in both personal and professional endeavors. Ultimately, the mission of a school is to empower students to achieve their full potential and make a positive impact on the world around them.

History

The Art School was established in 1927 by Patricio Fatima Marquessa Alejandro and has been a center of artistic education ever since. Its inception was marked by a commitment to fostering creativity and cultivating artistic skills in students. While the school has evolved over the years, its core mission has remained the same: to provide students with the hands-on training and critical thinking skills necessary to succeed in the world of art. Today, it is one of the most respected art schools in the country, attracting students from all over the world and producing some of the most talented artists of our time.
